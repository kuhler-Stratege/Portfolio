In autumn of 2021 I participated in a Gamejam organized by Ubisoft.
My team created a game where you need to solve puzzles with mables, where each marble makes a different sound.
I was responsible for the movement of the marbles. Each marble is receiving impuls forces from the player and then the marble needs to roll into the defined goal.
